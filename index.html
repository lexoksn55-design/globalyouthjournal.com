Empowering Young Minds, One Paper at a Time
GlobalYouthJournal is a youth-led initiative providing a FREE platform for student researchers and journalists to publish their work. Made by youth, for youth — with a student review process that values learning and accessibility.
About Us
The GlobalYouthJournal is a youth-led initiative that allows young, skilled researchers and journalists to publish their work without any fees — just a short student review period. This website exists for youth, made by youth. We aim to help you achieve your goals with minimal restrictions.
